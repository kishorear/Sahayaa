# Billing Support Guide

## Common Billing Issues

### Payment Processing Problems
1. **Credit Card Declined**
   - Verify card details are correct
   - Check with bank for any holds or restrictions
   - Try alternative payment method
   - Contact billing department if issue persists

2. **Invoice Discrepancies**
   - Review billing cycle dates
   - Check for prorated charges
   - Verify service usage reports
   - Escalate to billing manager for complex cases

3. **Refund Requests**
   - Verify refund eligibility based on terms of service
   - Process partial refunds for prorated services
   - Full refunds require manager approval
   - Document reason for refund in customer notes

### Account Management
- Update payment methods through customer portal
- Billing cycles can be adjusted with 30-day notice
- Late fees apply after 15-day grace period
- Account suspension occurs after 30 days overdue

### Escalation Guidelines
- Payment disputes over $500 require manager review
- Technical billing issues escalate to IT billing team
- Refund requests over $1000 need executive approval

Priority Level: HIGH for payment failures, MEDIUM for billing questions
